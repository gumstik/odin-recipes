Spaghetti

Spaghetti and meat (no balls). I love this and my mother served it all the time while I was growing up. The best part are the leftovers in my opinion. Once the noodles are stored together with the sauce for a day they soak it up and taste even better.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 1 whole onion
  • 1 bag spaghetti noodles
  • 3 cans of tomato sauce
  • half of a garlic clove

Step by step

  1. In the beginning, we start boiling the noodles.
  2. Next, we brown the meat in the pan until it's barely pink.
  3. Then dice the onion and garlic, then add it to the pan.
  4. After a few minutes, add in the cans of tomato sauce. Just like the stroganoff, we'll add in a can of water so it isn't so thick.
  5. Stir until bubbling. Serve with noodles first with meat sauce on top.
